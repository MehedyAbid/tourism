Hotel in Dhaka, Bangladesh

Situated on seven acres of lush gardens in the heart of Dhaka's primary commercial hub, the Radisson Blu Dhaka Water Garden provides a serene escape from the bustle of the city. Offering the perfect balance of supreme comfort and business-minded functionality, the hotel features five-star resort amenities and incredible conveniences, such as the short ten-minute drive to Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport (DAC). Major tourist attractions, such as the National Assembly Building or Ahsan Manzil, are also located nearby, making the hotel a perfect home base for both business and leisure trips to the Bangladesh capital.
